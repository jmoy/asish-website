# Asish K. Bhattacharyya
asish.bhattacharyya@nonlinear-insights.com

Prof. Asish K Bhattacharyya is a distinguished expert in Corporate Governance, Enterprise
Risk Management, Management Accounting, and Corporate Financial Reporting. He delivers
executive education programs and consulting services in these specialised domains.

In his illustrious career, Prof. Bhattacharyya held leadership positions for 20 years in
large corporations and dedicated 33 years to academia. His final industry role was as
Financial Controller of the Transformer Division at GEC Alstom Limited (India). In
academia, he has served as a Professor at renowned institutions, including IIM Calcutta,
SPJIMR Mumbai, and IICA (Manesar, Gurugram). He has also held the positions of
Technical Director at the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), Director at IMI
Kolkata and Director at IMT Ghaziabad.

A pioneer in his field, Prof. Bhattacharyya founded the Centre of Corporate
Governance at IIM Calcutta and led it for seven years. He was the inaugural Professor and
Head of the School of Corporate Governance and Public Policy at the Indian Institute of
Corporate Affairs (IICA, Manesar), a prominent think tank under the Ministry of Corporate
Affairs. Additionally, he contributed as a Senior Advisor at KPMG for three years.

His board experience includes serving as an independent director for a large public
sector enterprise (2008–2023), a listed private sector enterprise (2004–2021), and Escorts
Asset Management Limited (2001–2020), among others.
